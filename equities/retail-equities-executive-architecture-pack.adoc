== Purpose

This executive brief provides a board-friendly view of how the retail equities platform delivers secure digital trading, real-time market visibility, and reliable external execution.

It summarizes the operating model behind ETA (mobile channel), ETS (core trading platform), DZH market data, and broker connectivity.

== Business Outcomes

The architecture is designed to achieve five outcomes:

* *Customer confidence:* secure mobile access and resilient trading journeys
* *Execution quality:* dependable market and limit order processing
* *Market transparency:* live pricing, watchlist updates, and clear order status
* *Operational scalability:* one-to-many market data distribution for peak demand periods
* *Control and governance:* clear trust boundaries for internal and external connectivity

== High-Level Operating Model

At a high level, the platform operates in four business layers:

. *Digital channel layer:* ETA mobile app provides customer interaction and order capture.
. *Security and distribution layer:* requests are authenticated at secure entry points and live market data sessions are managed centrally.
. *Core trading layer:* ETS services perform business processing, order/risk handling, and market data orchestration.
. *Connectivity layer:* controlled extranet pathways connect to DZH for market data and to brokers for trade execution.

This layered approach supports strong controls without compromising customer responsiveness.

== Customer and Trading Capabilities

The executive capability set includes:

* Buy and sell using market and limit orders
* Live watchlist and ticker updates
* Order history and transaction visibility
* Stable post-logon streaming for market data consumption

== Market Data Strategy (Executive View)

The platform uses a consolidated market data strategy:

* ETA clients register a streaming session after logon
* Subscription intent is handled by core services
* Market data is ingested from DZH once per instrument path
* A centralized session hub fans out updates to all subscribed clients

Business value:

* Lower duplicate traffic across internal zones
* Better performance consistency during active market hours
* More efficient scaling as user concurrency grows

== Risk and Control Highlights

Key control measures are built into the deployment model:

* Segmented zones across channel, core platform, data, and extranet connectivity
* Security gateway controls for authentication and encrypted ingress
* Controlled external connectivity for market data and brokers
* Integrated order management and risk processing before execution
* Shared enterprise data platform for traceability and reporting

== Strategic Takeaways for Leadership

The current design supports near-term growth in retail trading volumes while maintaining strong operational controls.

It also provides a scalable foundation for future enhancements such as richer analytics, broader instrument coverage, and expanded digital engagement features.
